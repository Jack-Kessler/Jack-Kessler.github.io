Featured Project: Jack's Sushi Restaurant

My most recent "completed" project was designed using ASP.NET Core MVC including C#, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Dapper, and MySQL. The inspiration of this project came from -- you guessed it -- when I was eating sushi at my local go-to sushi spot. I used two different sushi restaurants (one in Japan and one in the US) where I often frequented as my starting base as I envisioned and built out this project.

I have created a video series below that takes the viewer through my project - including some parts of the underlying code, of course. The first two videos - one in English and the other in Japanese - are an introduction to the project in addition to explaining the login (authentication and authorization) features of the project. Each subsequent video - created in English - focuses on one of the five different user roles that the application supports: server, chef, manager, owner, and customer (guest).

I hope you enjoy!
